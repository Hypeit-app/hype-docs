# Earning on Hype

Hype has clear earning mechanics for creators and collectors.

## What you can do

- Earn as a creator when your post is collected
- Earn as an owner when someone buys from you
- Understand royalty mechanics on resales

## How earnings work

### Direct earnings (creator)

When someone collects your ownable post, you receive proceeds from that sale.

### Secondary market upside (owner)

If you currently own a moment and another user buys it from you, you receive proceeds from that sale.

### Creator royalties

On resales, creator royalty rules are applied through marketplace contract settings. The exact distribution can vary by network and contract configuration.

## What to check before posting

- Set a fair starting price.
- Make sure your post is clear and high quality.
- Make sure your opening price matches the quality and demand you expect.
