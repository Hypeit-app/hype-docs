# Notifications

Notifications help you react fast to activity that matters.

Push notifications require the Hype app to be installed first. If you have not installed it yet, follow [Install Hype App](/install-hype-app).

## What you can do

- Get notified when your content is collected
- Track follows, replies, and engagement
- Stay updated on listing and bid activity

## When you get notified

You get notifications when people follow you, interact with your posts, collect your content, or place listing and bid activity that needs your attention.

## How to set it up

1. Install Hype using [Install Hype App](/install-hype-app).
2. Open the installed app.
3. Scroll the feed until you see **Enable notification**.
4. Tap the **Enable notification** card.
5. In the device prompt, tap **Allow**.
6. (Optional) Connect Telegram alerts.
