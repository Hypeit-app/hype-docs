# Creator Playbook

This is the practical guide for creators who want better posts, better collectors, and better outcomes.

## What you can do

- Publish moments people want to collect
- Set better opening prices
- Build repeat collectors over time

## How to do it

### What makes a good Hype post

A good Hype post is easy to understand fast: the first frame grabs attention, the context is clear in one or two lines, and the post has a specific point of view instead of generic filler.

### How to set your opening price

Start with a price that feels fair for your audience size and content quality. If you are testing a new format, begin with a lower entry price to reduce friction, then raise pricing as demand and collector confidence grow.

### How creators build collectors

1. Post consistently.
2. Reply to comments quickly.
3. Explain why a moment matters.
4. Link collectors to related moments.

### Examples of good moments

- Behind-the-scenes from a launch
- A unique artwork drop
- A sharp meme with clear context
- A travel clip with story
