# Getting Started

This page gets you from zero to your first post.

## What you can do

- Create your account
- Install the Hype app for push notifications
- Connect your wallet
- Post your first moment
- Set your first price

## How to do it

### Step 1: Create your account

1. Open Hype.
2. Tap **Sign up**.
3. Choose a username you want to keep.

### Step 2: Connect your wallet

1. Open **Settings**.
2. Tap **Connect Wallet**.
3. Approve the wallet connection.

### Step 3: Install the Hype app (for push alerts)

1. Open [Install Hype App](/install-hype-app).
2. Follow iPhone or Android install steps.
3. Launch Hype from the installed app icon.

### Step 4: Post your first moment

1. Tap **Create**.
2. Add text, image, or video.
3. Tap **Post**.

### Step 5: Set your first price

1. Open your post.
2. Enter your price.
3. Review and publish.
