# What is Hype?

Hype is a social app where posts are collectible by default.

You can post something meaningful, set your price, and let collectors buy it.

## Why Hype exists

Most social posts disappear in the timeline.

Hype is built so strong moments can keep value after the first post.

## What makes Hype different

Hype combines social publishing and collecting in one flow. A post can be shared like normal content, but it can also become collectible. That means creators can earn when people collect their moments, and collectors can own the moments they care about.

## How posts become collectible

On Hype, your post is collectible from the moment you publish it. Collectors can buy it directly in the app, and after a successful purchase, ownership moves to the buyer and appears in that collector's collection.
