# Owning Posts (Collectors)

Owning a post means you collected that moment and it is now in your collection.

## What you can do

- Collect posts you like
- Track prices before collecting
- View everything you own in one place

## How to do it

### How to collect a post

1. Open a post that is ownable.
2. Tap **Own it**.
3. Confirm payment.
4. Wait for confirmation.

### How pricing works

Each ownable post shows a current price before you buy. That price can move over time based on activity and ownership changes, but you always see the final buy amount before you confirm the transaction.

### Viewing your collection

1. Open your profile.
2. Go to **Collection**.
3. Open any owned moment to view details.
