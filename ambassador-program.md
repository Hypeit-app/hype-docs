# Ambassador Program

The Ambassador Program is for people who can bring real creators into Hype and help them activate. Culture comes first, growth comes second.

## What you can do

- Invite high-quality creators who publish original work
- Help creators activate with real posting behavior
- Build long-term network effects, not short-term spikes

## Culture standard

Hype is building a creator ecosystem centered around real people, real stories, and real work. We are not building an AI content farm, a spam network, or a numbers-only game. If you represent Hype, you represent that standard.

## Who we are looking for

We onboard real creators, including people who publish original videos, photography, niche art, build-in-public updates, and real-world experiences. If the content would exist without AI tools, it fits. If content only exists because of AI prompting, it is not a fit.

## Program goal

Each ambassador has one clear objective: onboard **50 real creators** who each publish at least **3 posts** on Hype within **14 days**.

If someone signs up but does not post, they do not count. If they post low-effort or AI-generated spam, they do not count. Activation is the metric, not registrations.

## Minimum creator standards

Creators you onboard should be active in the last 7 days, usually have around 1k+ followers (flexible when quality is exceptional), and consistently produce original content with visible effort, personality, or craft.

## What Hype is not

Hype is not an AI image dump, a copy-paste Twitter mirror, an engagement-bait platform, or an airdrop hunting ground. If your strategy depends on those tactics, this program is not a fit.

## How referral links work

1. Set your username.
2. Open the ambassador/referral section.
3. Copy your referral link.
4. Share it with creators.

Referral format:

`https://hypeit.app/?ref=<your_username>`

## Ambassador perks (performance-based)

Ambassador status is earned through activation, not claimed through application. There are no upfront payments and no vague promises. Performance unlocks opportunity.

### Tier 1: Activated Ambassador

Unlocked after onboarding 50 activated creators.

- Public recognition from Hype
- Ambassador badge
- Early feature access
- Access to private strategy group

### Tier 2: Growth Ambassador

Unlocked after onboarding 150 activated creators.

- Revenue-share opportunities (future programs)
- Featured profile placement
- Direct collaboration on campaigns
- Priority ecosystem visibility

## Network effect expectation

Every creator you onboard should be encouraged to publish at least 3 times, then onboard 5 additional real creators. The goal is a self-sustaining ecosystem, not a temporary growth burst.

## Final note

We would rather onboard 20 exceptional creators than 200 low-effort accounts. If you care about long-term creator ownership and real culture, you are a fit for this program. If you are only chasing quick perks, this is not the right program.
