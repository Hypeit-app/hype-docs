# Referrals and Ambassadors

## How referrals work

Your referral link format:

`https://hypeit.app/?ref=<your_username>`

When a new user signs up with your username code, they are linked as your referral.

## Create your referral link

1. Set your username in profile.
2. Open referral section.
3. Tap **Get my link**.
4. Share link on social channels.

## Username requirements

- 3 to 20 characters
- Letters, numbers, and `_` only

## Top Ambassadors

The app shows a **Top ambassadors** block in feed.

Ranking is based on qualified referrals, including referral quality signals (for example connected social status).

## Tips to grow referrals

- Keep your username simple and memorable.
- Add your link in bio and pinned posts.
- Explain what new users can do first (post, own, earn).
