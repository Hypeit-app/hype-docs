# FAQ

## Can I delete an owned post?

If a post has already been collected, deletion may be limited by app rules.

## Do I keep rights to my content?

You keep your creator rights to your content. Ownership and usage rights for collectors follow platform and listing rules.

## Can collectors resell?

Yes. If resale is enabled for that moment, collectors can resell based on marketplace rules.

## Are posts NFTs?

Ownable moments are represented on-chain and behave like collectible digital assets.

## Why can’t I post?

Most common reasons:

- Required fields are missing
- Price is below minimum
- Media format is invalid
- Wallet/session issue

## Why can’t I collect a post?

Most common reasons:

- You already own it
- Post is no longer active
- Wallet/network mismatch
- Insufficient balance for value + gas
