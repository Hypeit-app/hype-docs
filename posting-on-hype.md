# Posting on Hype

Posting on Hype is how you publish moments and start building collectors.

## What you can do

- Publish text, image, and video posts
- Set price and publish collectible moments
- Edit or delete your posts

## How to do it

### Creating a post

1. Tap **Create**.
2. Add text, image, or video.
3. Review your post.
4. Tap **Post**.

### Setting price before publish

1. Add your post content.
2. Enter your price.
3. Review the post details.
4. Tap **Post**.

### Editing posts

1. Open your post.
2. Tap **Edit**.
3. Save changes.

### Deleting posts

1. Open your post.
2. Tap **Delete**.
3. Confirm.

If a post has already been collected, deletion behavior may be limited by app rules.
