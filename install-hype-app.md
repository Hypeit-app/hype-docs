# Install Hype App

If you want push notifications, install the Hype app first.

Push notifications are only available for users who have the app installed.

## Why this matters

Without the installed app, you can still use Hype, but you will not receive push alerts for follows, collects, replies, or listing activity.

## How to install

### iPhone (Safari)

1. Open `https://hypeit.app` in Safari.

![Enable notification card in feed](/images/install-hype-app-card.png)

2. Tap **Share**.

![iOS share menu](/images/share-menu.png)

3. Tap **Add to Home Screen**.

![Add to Home Screen highlighted](/images/add-to-home-screen.png)

4. Open Hype from your home screen icon.

![Hype app installed on phone](/images/hype-app-installed.png)

### Android (Chrome)

1. Open `https://hypeit.app` in Chrome.
2. Tap the browser menu.
3. Tap **Install app** or **Add to Home screen**.
4. Open Hype from your app icon.

## Turn on push notifications

1. Open the installed Hype app.
2. Scroll the feed until you see the **Enable notification** card.
3. Tap the card.
4. When permission popup appears, tap **Allow**.

## Troubleshooting

- If you do not see the install option, update your browser and try again.
- If push is still off, check device notification permissions for Hype.
- If alerts are delayed, open the app once to refresh your session.
